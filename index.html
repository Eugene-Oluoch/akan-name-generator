AKAN NAME GENERATOR

This is a web app that takes your birthday details and calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender it outputs your Akan Name.

This idea was driven from Ghanian culture where children are given their first names as the 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
